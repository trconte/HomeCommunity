Community Helpers Services Center

United to Help Each Other

At the heart of Knightdale, NC

Billy Ray Neal founded Community Helpers Services Center in 1996, dedicated to supporting local families and individuals in need. Since then it has grown into a bustling community of helpers. We provide food, clothing, and other essential resources, offering a helping hand to those facing hardship. Our goal is to strengthen the community by ensuring that everyone has access to basic necessities and support when they need it most.
